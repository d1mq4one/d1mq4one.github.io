Inspired By Metro.

Drawing inspiration from Microsoft's Metro design language, MetroTwit celebrates an elegant and clean user interface that's beautiful to look at and a pleasure to read.

Made for .

Taking advantage of the latest Microsoft .NET and Windows Presentation Foundation frameworks, MetroTwit is a first-class application that shines on Windows.

Tweet with me, myself, and I.

With multiple-accounts support, quickly and easily switch between different personas for work or fun - view and tweet as if you were one. (MetroTwit Plus feature)
